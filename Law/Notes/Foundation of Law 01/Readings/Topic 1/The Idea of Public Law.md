Where law gets it power and how it balances government control?

### Sovereignty and “Exception”

- **The Idea:** Real power is shown when a leader makes a decision during crisis that goes **outside** the normal rules
- **The Problem:** If a leader ignores the law too often, people stop believing they are a fair or legal ruler.

### Why do they Obey?

#### Under Natural law

Law should be based on **morality and reason**. If a law is truely evil, it isn’t a real law.

#### Under Legal Positivism

Law is just a **social fact.** If the person in charge says it’s a rule and people follow it, it’s a law-regardless of whether it’s good or bad.

### The Goal of Public Law

Public law is like a scale. It has to:

1. **Give power** to the government so they can run the country
2. **Set limits** on that power (the **Rule of Law**) So the government doesn’t become a tyranny.

### The Social Contract

This is the deal at the heart of society. People agree to follow a government’s rules in exchange for **protection and order**.
