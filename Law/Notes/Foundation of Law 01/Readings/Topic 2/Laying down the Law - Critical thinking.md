**Critical Thinking** is a key for law students. Instead of just memorising, you must actively question and analyse information.

### Main points to check:

- **Validity:** Are facts true and supported by evidence?
- **Author/Speaker:** Are they credible, expert, or biased?
- **Context:** Is it relevant now (especially in Australia)?
- **Values:** What beliefs or principles lie behind it?
- **Assumptions:** What unstated beliefs is it based on? (e.g. harsher penalties will reduce crime)
- **Implications:** What are the consequences or possible problems?

**Bottom line:** Don’t be afraid to respectfully question facts, laws, and arguments to develop deeper understanding.
