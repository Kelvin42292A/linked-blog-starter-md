High Court of Australia
## Material Facts

- Zaburoni knew he was HIV positive
- He had a sexual relationship with the complainant and repeatedly had unprotected sex with her over a long period **without disclosing** his HIV status
- He persuaded her to have unprotected sex despite knowing the risks
- After the complainant test HIV positive, Zaburoni lied to her and the police about his knowledge of his condition
- He was convicted of transmitting a serious disease **with intent** (Criminal Code Qlds 317(1)(b)) and appealed to the High Court.

## Issues

Whether the prosecution proved **beyond reasonable doubt** that Zaburoni **intended** to transmit HIV to the complainant (specific intent), or whether his conduct only showed **recklessness.**

## Rule/Law

- Under [Criminal Code (Qld), s 317(1)(b)](https://anzlaw.thomsonreuters.com/Link/Document/FullText?refType=U5&docFamilyGuid=I89f2d1fbe9b011eb808c9e34970b55bb&pubNum=1100190&originationContext=document&transitionType=DocumentItem&docVersion=Law%20in%20Force&ppcid=6419281bc31c407ba0c962915ad5f235&contextData=\(sc.Default\)&comp=wlau), it is an offence to unlawfully transmit a serious disease **with intent** to transmit it
- **Intention** requires to proof that the accused **meant** (had the purpose) to cause the specific result (transmission of HIV)
- **Foresight** or **recklessness** (knowing there was a risk) is **not enough** to prove intention under the Code

## Application/Analysis

- The evidence showed Zaburoni **knew** he had HIV and took a serious risk by having frequent unprotected sex without tell the complainant
- His lies to the complainant and police showed he wanted to **hide** his HIV status and deceive her
- However this is only proved **recklessness** (he knew there was a risk of transmission), **not** that he **intended** (had a purpose) to transmit HIV to her.
- The prosecution could not prove specific intent beyond reasonable doubt

## Conclusion

- The High Court allowed the appeal
- Zaburoni was **not guilty** of transmitting HIV **with intent** (s 317(1)(b))
- The evidence only proved recklessness, not specific intent.
